Requesting Transcripts

  1. Determine which school(s) require transcripts to be sent.
  2. Remember, UCs and CSUs do not need transcripts, unless a campus has specifically contacted you and requested your transcript.
  3. Log onto Naviance Family Connection.
  4. Click on the Colleges tab.
  5. Click on the Manage Transcripts link.
  6. Print a screenshot of this confirmation page and write your name and student ID number on it.Â (It will look identical to the initial request you submitted to the Counseling Office when you requested your initial transcripts.)
  7. Highlight the schools for which you need transcripts to be sent.
  8. Submit your paper (with highlighted schools) to the Counseling Office.
Please see your counselor if you have questions!
