Naviance Family Connection

Using Naviance Family Connection you will be able to:

  1. Keep track of the process
    • Build a résumé
    • Complete career surveys
    • Manage timelines and deadlines for making decisions about colleges and careers
  2. Research colleges
    • Compare GPA scores
    • Compare SAT or ACT scores
    • Learn about admissions, student life, and academics
  3. Sign up for college visits
    • Find out which colleges are visiting our school
    • Sign up to attend the information sessions on campus
  4. Research scholaships
    • Apply for local and national opportunities listed under the "Scholarship List"
Naviance Family Connection also lets us share information with you about upcoming meetings, events, local scholarship opportunities, and other web resources for college and career information.

Logging into Naviance

  1. Go to the SRVUSD Portal
  2. Log in with your school email (dh.____@students.srvusd.net)
  3. Click on Student Applications
  4. Click on Naviance
